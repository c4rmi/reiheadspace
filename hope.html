1

Today I made my bed
And life started feeling less heavy
I could use a thousand adjectives
To describe how I feel
And you still wouldn't understand
Maybe the reason I like the color blue
Is because I am sad

2

the lifeless soul
that takes over my body
it consumes me
and I let it

I let it
I let everyone go
I let myself go
to die
and to live
countless times

3

the screams within the back of my mind
was so loud and deafening
but as I drew closer and closer
and closer
to snapping

I found myself
unable to release it all
enduring everything instead
all my rage was hidden
inside my laugh

4

the anger i have towards the world
is just as heavy
as the love i want to give

5

It is both
A blessing and a curse
To be able to feel
Because if I only could
Drown myself in utter silence
I'd chose eternal peace
Rather than to live
In a world where I was constantly

Misunderstood

6

the thought of not belonging somewhere
pricks my heart and squeezes its tears
because to be an island
is to be isolated
and to be isolated
is to be ignored
